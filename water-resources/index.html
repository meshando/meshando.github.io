Some Recent Work

01

Landfill Hydrology & Drainage

$40,000 EREF Study Deep Dive

I led a research initiative looking into exactly how stormwater infiltration impacts the moisture conditions of landfill covers. It's crucial because moisture drives methane oxidation. We had to figure out how efficient the drainage networks actually were under heavy load.

Python, ArcGIS Pro, Civil 3D
02

Green Infrastructure Master Plan

Jacksonville, FL Design

Urban flooding is a massive issue in Florida. I helped design a comprehensive master plan that integrated green infrastructure—like bioswales and permeable pavements—to handle peak stormwater runoff before it overwhelms the gray infrastructure.

EPA SWMM, HEC-RAS, GIS
03

Floodplain Delineation

FEMA Compliance Mapping

I ran 1D and 2D hydraulic models to update out-of-date floodplain maps. By combining high-res LiDAR data with HEC-RAS, we got incredibly accurate inundation boundaries that are now being used for local zoning compliance.

HEC-RAS 2D, LiDAR, AutoCAD
04

Culvert & Pipe Sizing Automation

Internal Tool Scripting

I got tired of doing manual pipe sizing calculations, so I wrote a Python script that pulls watershed data directly from GIS, calculates the rational method runoff, and spits out the optimized pipe and culvert sizes automatically.

Python, Pandas, ArcPy

The Tools I Use

Hydraulic Modeling

HEC-RAS (1D & 2D) and EPA SWMM. This is where I simulate the actual flow of water through rivers and pipes.

Design & Drafting

Civil 3D and AutoCAD. Crucial for turning raw calculations into actual buildable plans and profiles.

Spatial Analysis

ArcGIS Pro. Everything happens somewhere. I use GIS to analyze watersheds, slopes, and land use.
