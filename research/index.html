Research Areas

01

FOD Models & Measurement

Emissions Analysis

Comparison of landfill methane First-Order Decay (FOD) models with measurement-derived emissions to identify discrepancies and propose empirically-backed calibration factors for national greenhouse gas inventories.

FOD Models | Statistical Modeling
02

SEM Inverse Modeling

Algorithm Development

SEM-to-emission-rate inverse modeling utilizing genetic algorithms and dispersion techniques. This novel approach transforms compliance concentration monitoring into quantifiable mass emission rates.

Inverse Modeling | Genetic Algorithms
03

PFAS Dispersion

Atmospheric Transport

Evaluating PFAS atmospheric dispersion and exposure screening. Integrating meteorological data and emission factors to model the transport and deposition of persistent 'forever chemicals' from waste sources.

Gaussian Dispersion | Risk Assessment
04

Landfill Drainage & Oxidation

System Performance

Investigating landfill drainage, internal moisture dynamics, and their integrated effect on methane oxidation performance in biocover systems.

Hydrologic Modeling | Biogeochemistry
